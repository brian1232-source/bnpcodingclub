About us

Why we meet every week

01

Learn by shipping

Every member ships at least one real project per term — a site, a script, a small app — instead of just following tutorials.

02

Peer-taught

Members who know HTML/CSS, Python, or Java run short sessions. Teaching it is how you actually learn it.

03

Compete together

We prepare as a team for competitions like KATTI, pairing strong coders with beginners so everyone grows and solve some problem.

Upcoming sessions

Beginners

HTML & CSS

JavaScript

JavaScript Fundamentals & DOM Manipulation

AI Skills

Deploy your projects

Hosting & Deployment Platforms

Static Sites

  • GitHub Pages — Free hosting for HTML/CSS/JS projects
  • Netlify — Easy deployment with continuous builds
  • Vercel — Perfect for Next.js and modern frontends

Backend Hosting

  • Heroku — Simple Node.js, Python, PHP deployment
  • Railway — Modern platform for web apps
  • Render — Free tier for side projects

Club members get mentorship on choosing and deploying to the right platform for your project type.
